About Prairie Operating Co.

Prairie Operating Co. is at the forefront of a transformative movement in manufacturing, combining the best of biology and chemistry to produce new, essential, and valuable chemicals. Our mission is to revolutionize the way the world produces fuels, foods, medicines, and other critical products, moving away from unsustainable and inefficient manufacturing processes.
